# IronScout Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** TBD

## 1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how IronScout collects, uses, and protects information. IronScout is a US-only service at launch.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Email address
- Account credentials
- Saved items and alert preferences
- Optional context such as calibers you shoot

### Information We Do Not Collect
IronScout does not collect firearm serial numbers, purchase history, proof of ownership, or payment card data.

## 3. Automatically Collected Information
We may collect IP address, browser type, device information, and usage events for security, reliability, and analytics.

## 4. How We Use Information
Information is used to operate and maintain the service, monitor prices and availability, deliver alerts, improve relevance, and prevent abuse. We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking
IronScout uses cookies for authentication, session management, and analytics. You can control cookies through browser settings.

## 6. Sharing of Information
We may share limited information with analytics providers, infrastructure providers, and affiliate partners (click-level only). We do not share personal identity with retailers.

## 7. Data Retention
We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the service or comply with legal obligations. You may delete your account at any time.

## 8. Security
We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No system is perfectly secure.

## 9. Your Rights
You may access, update, or request deletion of your account information.

## 10. Children's Privacy
IronScout is not intended for users under 18.

## 11. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be posted clearly.

## 12. Contact
Questions may be directed to legal@ironscout.com.
