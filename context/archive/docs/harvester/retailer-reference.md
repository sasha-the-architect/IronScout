For harvester development and testing:

1. **Large catalogs** (10k+ products): Sentry Ammo, Ammo ASAP, Ammo Hero, Fast Ammo - good for stress testing
2. **Medium catalogs** (1-5k products): Most mid-tier retailers - realistic production loads
3. **Small catalogs** (<100 products): Manufacturer directs, specialty shops - quick iteration
